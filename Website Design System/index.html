Introduction

When designing a website design continuity is important. In order to achieve this it is necessary to keep certain aspects of the design, such as the colour palettes or typography used, uniform throughout the site. Many websites have their own unique style and colourings - think Wikipedia or Google - which are iconic and synonymous to them.

This webpage one of the Codecademy projects to aid the future design of webpages that I make by displaying some attractive design aspects that I can draw from later.

It can be quite difficult to pick out colours that complement eachother, but a good resource to use which automatically picks complementary shades is Coolors.co.

Alternatively, Colormind is also a color matching site which gives a more detailed suggestion of where and how to apply its recommendations.

Font Families

Another important aspect of webpage and website design is the use of font families. Often the default browser font is fairly basic, and having a variation of similar fonts improves appeal and readability.
